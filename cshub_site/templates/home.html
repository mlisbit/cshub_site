Who are we?

We are the Computing Students Hub, a club founded at York University, Toronto. Don't let the name fool you, we have members from all faculties - if you're interested in Technology, and want to meet upw with some cool people who are too? this is the place for you.


What's there to do in the CSHub?

We are a relatively new club, just starting September 2013, so we're not entirely sure ourselves! But what we have planned so far is pretty cool; meetups, free food, tutorial sessions for classes, programming tutorials, seminars.


That's great! is there any way I can get involved?

Yeah! Whether you only want to attend the events, or help out in organization, and planning for the club - there's a spot for you. Your first step is to sign up for an account, this confirms your position in the club, and will allow us to easily reach out and contact you. Next step: contact us.
